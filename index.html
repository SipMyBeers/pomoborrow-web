How it works

Three steps.
Zero effort.

ACE — the Autonomous Context Engine — runs silently in the background and figures out the rest.

Step 01 ⌚

Wear it

Open the app once. Grant location access. Then forget about it. The ring begins tracking your entire day, second by second, from midnight.

Step 02 🔁

Live your life

ACE silently learns your places, your motion, your patterns. It detects your gym, your office, your commute — without you telling it anything.

Step 03 🔍

Review and refine

Glance at your day ring each evening. Tap any segment to correct a label. Every correction trains ACE to be more accurate tomorrow.
