FREQUENTLY ASKED

Questions we get a lot.

Is it really 100% offline? +

Yes. PomoBorrow has no server. No cloud. No account. Everything runs and stays on your device. We literally cannot see your data even if we wanted to — the architecture makes it impossible.

Does it drain my battery? +

Less than 5% per day. PomoBorrow uses event-driven detection (not GPS polling) and Apple's motion coprocessor — a dedicated chip that runs at near-zero power. We also have a tri-tier power management system that automatically throttles when your battery is low.

How long until it learns my routine? +

48 hours. Day 1 is silent — it just watches. Day 2 it asks ~5 simple questions ("Is this your office?"). By day 5 it's running on autopilot with 90%+ accuracy. Every correction makes it smarter.

Does it listen to my conversations? +

No. The sound sensor classifies ambient noise categories (keyboard, gym equipment, traffic) using Apple's on-device Neural Engine. It never records, stores, or transmits audio. It can't hear words — only sound types.

How is this different from Apple Screen Time? +

Screen Time only tracks apps — it has no idea what you're doing in the physical world. PomoBorrow tracks your entire life: gym, commute, work, sleep, meals, errands. And it does it automatically using 5 sensor types, not just app usage.

What happens to my data if I delete the app? +

It's gone. Completely. Since everything is stored on-device, deleting the app deletes all your data. You can also export your full history as JSON before deleting, or wipe specific data from Settings at any time.
